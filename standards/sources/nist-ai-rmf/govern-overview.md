# NIST AI Risk Management Framework — Govern function (public overview)

The **Govern** function of the NIST AI RMF cultivates a culture of AI risk management within organizations. It establishes policies, processes, procedures, and practices to help organizations map, measure, and manage AI risks.

Key governance activities include:

- Defining roles and responsibilities for AI risk management across the organization
- Integrating AI risk management into broader enterprise risk and compliance programs
- Establishing accountability structures endorsed by senior leadership
- Ensuring workforce training on organizational AI policies and acceptable use
- Conducting due diligence on third-party AI components before integration

Governance should be iterative and informed by feedback from internal and external stakeholders, including affected communities where appropriate.
