# ISO/IEC 42001 — AI management system (public overview)

ISO/IEC 42001 specifies requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an **AI management system (AIMS)** within the context of an organization.

The standard is structured around familiar management-system clauses:

- **Context of the organization** — Understanding internal and external issues, interested parties, and scope of the AIMS
- **Leadership** — Top management commitment, AI policy, and defined roles
- **Planning** — Risk and opportunity identification, AI objectives, and planning of changes
- **Support** — Resources, competence, awareness, communication, and documented information
- **Operation** — Operational planning and control for AI-related processes
- **Performance evaluation** — Monitoring, internal audit, and management review
- **Improvement** — Nonconformity, corrective action, and continual improvement

Organizations certified to ISO/IEC 42001 demonstrate systematic governance of AI risks aligned with business objectives and regulatory expectations.
